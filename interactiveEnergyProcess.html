Interactive Song Energy Mapping Project

Project Overview

This project was created as a showcase of the different variations and subgenres of House Music. Using the subjective term of 'energy', different levels were categorized in each song. A systematic approach was used to variate the types of songs presented. Extensive research was also prevelant, as community feedback was also done in the project.

Many peers were interviewed and surveyed to gain more understanding of outside groups. More than 20 friends and family surveyed their own personal energy levels from 1-100 and used in the project. This feedback gave opportunity for more community interactivity, and therefore convinced more individuals about house music's variation.

This project was ideated from the prototyping software Figma, then being coded from the Javascript library P5js. Creating this project from the ground up left substantial opportunity for creative freedom and ideation. Although simply using a prototyping software could be applicable, having complete control of the project left room for the composition's main concept: energy and movement. In order to completely visualize this, coding the project from scratch was vital in order to illustrate the object's randomness, sound, and continuous movement.
