Mashouf Wellness Center App Redesign

Project Overview

As a student and typical gym-goer at San Francisco State University, the Mashouf Wellness Center is a regular destination for all recreational needs. However, even though the Wellness Center's application carries the essential functionalities, its visual elements and navigation had potential for a rework.

The Mashouf Wellness Center Redesign could be a great implementation to its current iteration. The redesign allows for greater legibility, easier navigation, and more affordances. This app potentially could become a hub for recreation for all students at San Francisco State, therefore improving student life altogether.

The Original App

Mashouf Wellness Center's currect recreational app is functional, but its interface could be organized differently. All of its icons are present in one screen, causing potential information overload. Redoing its information architecture was vital in the redesign.

Premise

My goal was to reimagine the Homepage and Navigation of the SFSU Mashouf Wellness Center to allow for for greater accessibility and usage.

Competitor Analysis

The Wellness Center App help many simliarities towards other gym apps like Planet Fitness, and 24 Hour Fitness's 24toGo. Both have a personal and intimate experience towards its users. However, it was discovered that both apps attempted to convince users to upgrade to a premium service for an extra fee. This gave opportunity for the MWC app because its audience are already admitted San Francisco State students.

Iterations

The main focus of this redesign was to create a personal and comfortable experience with the user. Using color schemes, typography, and banners from San Francisco State's Identity System, it was possible to match the desktop-site to the app's redesign.

Conclusion

The redesign treaded towards a feed-like interface that updated students on Mashouf Wellness Center's events and news. The addition of micro-interactions also called for a friendlier experience, while the hamburger menu sustained information architecture. Increased focus on its Intramural (REC) and Classes tabs also allowed for usability, as these two aspects are a large part of the Wellness Center

The Mashouf Wellness Center redesign could be a great implementation to its current iteration. The redesign allows for greater legibility, easier navigation, and more affordances. The app potentially could become a hub for recreation for all its students at San Francisco State, therefore improving student life altogether.
